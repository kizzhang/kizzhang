# Attribution and physical references

The black-hole geometry is derived from the AART Schwarzschild-limit simulation and is re-quantized into ASCII glyph masks.

- AART source (MIT): <https://github.com/iAART/aart>
- AART paper: <https://arxiv.org/abs/2211.07469>
- NASA Schwarzschild accretion-disk visualization: <https://svs.gsfc.nasa.gov/13326/>
- Gralla, Holz & Wald on photon/lensing rings: <https://arxiv.org/abs/1906.00873>
- Bruneton non-rotating black-hole renderer: <https://github.com/ebruneton/black_hole_shader>

The green palette is an artistic false color. The shadow, direct disk, lensed far side, underside, fixed critical contour, Doppler asymmetry, and exterior material-flow narrative remain distinct.
